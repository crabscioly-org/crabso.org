# Registration is now open for the 2026 CrabSO Invitational!

The in-person 2026 CrabSO Invitational will be held on **Saturday, January 24th, 2026** at Clarksville Middle School. It will cost $110 per team with a 3 team per school cap.

# In-person Competition

{: .notice--info}
**When:** January 24th, 2026

{: .notice--info}
**Where:**  
Clarksville Middle School  
6535 South Trotter Road  
Clarksville, Maryland, United States 21029

{: .notice--success}
**Cost:**  
$110 per team (3 teams per school cap).  
Title I schools: email [crabscioyl@gmail.com](mailto:crabscioyl@gmail.com) with proof of Title I status by **December 22, 2025 @ 11:59 PM EST** for fee waiver.

{: .notice--warning}
**Capacity:**  
40 teams — first come, first serve.

{: .notice--danger}
**Registration Deadline:**  
December 22, 2025 @ 11:59 PM EST, *or when capacity is reached*, whichever comes first.  
Payments (checks, cash, Scilympiad Paypal) must be dated by the deadline to count as fully registered.

{: .notice--primary}
### Note  
If in-person registration is full, email  
[crabscioyl@gmail.com](mailto:crabscioyl@gmail.com)  
to join the **first-come, first-serve waitlist**.

Waitlist movement will continue until  
**January 12th, 2026 @ 11:59 PM EST**,  
if any registered teams drop out.
